Every game has a competitive scene, whether it be in-person events or online battles, they are all exciting to watch.

Rocket League is no different, offering the best cinematic moments in gaming history, while also requiring the best of the

best players who compete in tournaments in LAN in front of thousands of people spectating. This championship is known

as RLCS (also known as Rocket League Championship Series), known for having winning prizes of up to $5,000,000 on

average, making it one of the most high-earning prize games in Esports history. RLCS is a worldwide event, having

people from all over the world compete in super fast-paced action.
